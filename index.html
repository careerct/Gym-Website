FREQUENTLY ASKED

Got Questions?

What are Fitness Zone's gym timings? +
Fitness Zone is open 7 days a week. Weekday hours: 5AM–10PM. Weekend hours: 6AM–9PM. Premium members get extended access.
Where is Fitness Zone located? +
We are located at Emerald Compound, Kovaipudur Main Rd, Kovaipudur, Tamil Nadu 641008 — near Kovaipudur village center. Easy parking available.
Do you offer a free trial? +
Yes! Contact us at +91 98946 10604 or fill our contact form to book a free trial session. No commitment required.
Is there a women's-only section? +
Yes, we have a dedicated women's workout zone with female trainers available. Women can train comfortably at Fitness Zone.
What payment methods do you accept? +
We accept UPI (GPay, PhonePe, Paytm), Debit/Credit Cards, Net Banking, and cash payments at the gym.
Can I pause my membership? +
Standard and Premium members can pause their membership for medical reasons for up to 30 days per year. Contact us to arrange.
